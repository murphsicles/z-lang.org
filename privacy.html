Privacy Notice

We respect your privacy

The Zeta Foundation operates z-lang.org and related services. We collect minimal data necessary for operation.

Data Collection

  • IP addresses and browser info are logged temporarily for security and performance.
  • No personal data is collected unless you provide it (e.g., GitHub login for discussions).
  • Cookies are used only for essential site functionality.
  • No tracking or analytics cookies are used.

Third Parties

GitHub hosts repositories and discussions; their privacy policy applies there.

Your Rights

Contact foundation@z-lang.org for data requests or concerns.
